About AfroCut

AfroCut is a modern digital platform designed to simplify the process of booking barber appointments, especially tailored for the Black community. With AfroCut, users can explore a variety of hairstyle inspirations, schedule appointments with trusted barbers, and manage payments — all in one place. Our mission is to make grooming accessible, reliable, and effortless, whether you're at home or on the go.
